PHOTO GALLERY SLIDER

This website showcases some of available features you can expect from me.

Features include, but are not limited to:

  • Auto-slide: The Banner image automatically changes every timeframe. Can be added on slider as well.
  • Random-order: Something you might or might not need. On page reload, the order of images is randomized.
  • Album-view: Preview what is in the album by hovering over each album, and click to expand the album.
  • Thumbnail-view: You can see thumbnails of images and click on them to navigate to specific image.
  • Fullscreen-view: Click on active image to see it in full-screen mode.
  • Mobile-friendly: Responsive to all devices, and simply swipe to slide the images.
